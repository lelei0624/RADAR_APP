Dashboard Overview

The Marketing Intelligence Dashboard tracks the MKT Inbound funnel end-to-end — from pipeline build-up (TMRR) through conversion to closed-won revenue. All views filter on department_source_clean = 'MKT Inbound' and exclude renewal deals.

Metric Definitions
MQL TMRR
Theoretical MRR — sum of line item MRR for active pipeline deals projected to close in a future month. Covers stages: Appointment Scheduled, Initial Demo Done, Solutioning, Commercial Negotiation, Committed Accounts. Deals with a past projected close date return 0 (slipped).
mrr_in_pipeline · projected_close_date
SQL TMRR
Same as MQL TMRR but restricted to later-stage deals: Solutioning, Commercial Negotiation, Committed Accounts. Represents pipeline that has passed initial qualification.
mrr_in_pipeline · projected_close_date
Won MRR vs Target
Sum of MRR for deals in Customer stage (closed-won), excluding renewals. Compared against monthly targets. The progress bar turns green ≥100%, yellow ≥80%, pink below 80%.
won_mrr · close_date
EmFi MRR
Embedded Finance MRR from won deals. Subset of Won MRR attributable to embedded financial products (e.g. EWA, loans, insurance).
won_ef_mrr · close_date
Closed-Won Revenue
Total deal amount for all won deals in the period. Includes one-time and recurring components (won_amount), not just MRR.
won_amount · close_date
Avg Deal Value
Average MRR per won deal: won_mrr ÷ won_deals. Useful for tracking deal size trends and mix shift between SME and ENT.
avg_deal_size · close_date
Deals Velocity
Average number of days from deal creation to close for won deals. Only includes deals with an availed product (joined to silver_line_items where product_status = 'Availed').
avg_deal_velocity · close_date
MQL → SQL Rate
Percentage of qualified leads (qualified_lead = 'Yes') that progressed past MQL stage (client_journey_stage ≠ 'MQL'). Both counts are based on create_date.
sql_pct = sales_qualified_leads ÷ qualified_leads · create_date
SQL → Won Rate
Win rate of deals that reached SQL stage: won_deals ÷ (won_deals + lost_deals). Based on create_date to follow the cohort story.
win_rate · create_date
Won MRR & EmFi MRR (create_date)
Won MRR and EmFi MRR attributed to deals created in the selected period (cohort view). Differs from the KPI cards above which use close_date. Used alongside SQL → Won to tell the complete funnel story.
won_mrr, won_ef_mrr · create_date
Micro vs SME Ratio SME view only
Distribution of won deals between Micro and SME sub-segments within the SME view. Sourced from segment mapping in silver_deals.
segment · create_date
TMRR Per Product Upsell view only
Won MRR broken down by product line (HR & Payroll, Employee Benefits, Workforce, Time & Attendance, etc.) for the PH Upsell Pipeline with MKT Inbound source.
won_mrr by product · close_date

Data Sources
gold_monthly_exec_summary
Pre-aggregated gold table rebuilt daily. Feeds Won MRR, EmFi MRR, CW Rev, Avg Deal Value, Velocity (all close_date), plus the monthly trend chart and MQL/SQL counts.
shared.revops.gold_monthly_exec_summary
gold_targets
Monthly quota targets by segment and pipeline. Feeds the MQL TMRR vs Target and Won MRR vs Target progress bars and percentage attainment.
shared.revops.gold_targets
silver_deals
Deal-level table from HubSpot. Source for MQL/SQL funnel counts, conversion rates, Won MRR (create_date cohort), EmFi MRR (create_date cohort), and the MQL by Channel Source chart. Filtered by department_source_clean = 'MKT Inbound'.
shared.revops.silver_deals
silver_line_items
Line-item MRR for each deal. Joined with silver_deals to compute MQL TMRR and SQL TMRR — summing mrr_in_pipeline for active pipeline stages with a future projected_close_date.
shared.revops.silver_line_items
silver_company
Company / account attributes (industry, employee count, region). Used for enrichment and segment-level breakdowns. Referenced in the SME Micro vs SME ratio and ENT deal profiling.
shared.revops.silver_company
gold_metrics_datapedia
Metric definitions and business glossary maintained by the RevOps team. Provides official definitions for all KPIs shown in this dashboard. Referenced as the source of truth for any metric-naming discrepancies.
